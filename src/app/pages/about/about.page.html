Lock Haven Football History

Lock Haven University has a long, rich history of football tradition, dating back to the teams beginnings in 1900. Many of the early teams had great success over the years, with several Pennsylvania State Teachers College Football Championships along with helping establish the Pennsylvania State Athletic Conference (PSAC)

LHU Football Team in 1940


Current Team

Lock Haven's football team finished with a 1-10 record under headcoach John Kelling this previous season. New headcoach Dan Mulrooney has taken Kelling's place and will be leading the Bald Eagles into the 2023-2024 season
